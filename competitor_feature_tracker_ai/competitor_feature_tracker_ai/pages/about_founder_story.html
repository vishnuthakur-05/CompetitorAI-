The Problem That Started It All

Every product manager knows the pain: spending hours researching competitors, only to have the insights outdated by the time you present them.

The Frustration

Manual Research Hell

Spending 10+ hours weekly browsing competitor websites, reading release notes, and trying to piece together strategic insights from scattered information.

Outdated by Presentation Time

By the time you compile insights into a deck, competitors have already shipped new features, making your analysis immediately stale.

Enterprise Tools Too Expensive

Existing competitive intelligence platforms cost $50K+ annually, putting them out of reach for startups and small product teams.

"There has to be a better way"
